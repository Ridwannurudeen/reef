Verify it yourself

Don't trust us — check the chain

  1. Open any contract link above. Mantlescan shows the verified Solidity source, the ABI, and the deployment transaction — verified via Etherscan V2, no unverified bytecode.

  2. Match a receipt's evidence hash. Take the rationale text shown in the decision feed and compute keccak256(text). It equals the evidenceHash field in the agent's EIP-712 signed receipt on-chain. The AI can't claim one thing and record another.

  3. Follow a swap transaction. Where a decision produced a trade, the "real swap" link resolves to a FusionX V2 DEX swap on Mantlescan with the full call trace and token transfers.

A vault whose NAV is a live DEX position

The seeded leaderboard vaults accrue via a testnet yield mock. This standalone vault is different: it deposits its reserve into a real FusionX V2 position, so its nav() is the live on-chain mark-to-market of that position — not a simulated number. Every contract below is Mantlescan-verified.

Observed on-chain: nav() = 0.99683 — exactly 1.0 minus the real ~0.32% round-trip trading cost of opening the position. The mark tracks the market because it's quoted live from the pool (getAmountsOut), never hardcoded.

Scope & honesty

What this is — and what it isn't

  • Testnet only. Everything here runs on Mantle Sepolia (chain 5003). There is no mainnet deployment and no real TVL.
  • Yield is simulated. Sepolia uses a MockYieldAdapter that accrues NAV linearly on-chain. The per-share growth is real on the testnet, but it is simulated yield — not returns from a live strategy.
  • RWA tokens are referenced, not deployed. Real USDY/mETH and other Mantle RWA assets are mainnet-ready and pinned for reference below, but their adapters are not deployed on Sepolia. The testnet index asset is a mock ERC-20.
  • Unaudited. These contracts have not been audited. This page is a transparency demonstration, not financial advice and not an invitation to deposit real funds.
