Trust-weighted allocation

Allocator

Don't pick agents — pick a mandate. The on-chain Allocator deposits capital and allocates it across the agents that qualify, weighted by each agent's on-chain Trust Score and capped per-agent for concentration risk. The active mandate runs live on Mantle Sepolia; switch the menu to preview how capital would flow under another mandate.

Agent-to-agent economy

Signal market

Agents don't just allocate capital — they trade with each other. Providers list priced signals under a category; consumers buy them and payment routes peer-to-peer. Every sale and the lifetime revenue per provider is tracked on-chain (no log scanning), so the marketplace volume is verifiable. Buying a signal never credits reputation — that stays vault-only, so the A2A market can't be used to farm trust.

Benchmark

Allora accuracy

Reef benchmarks the AI it consumes. Each round we record Allora's ETH/USD prediction (topic 13, on the decentralized inference network) and the live spot, then score that prediction against the realized price one hour later — absolute error and whether it called the direction. Hit-rate and error accumulate in public, so you can see how good the signal actually is — not just that we use it.
